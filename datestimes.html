Dates And Times

The dates and times for the beginning of the event and timetable for each day are shown below.

  • The beginning of the Cyclemon event takes place on the 16th May.
  • It will start at the University Of Greenwich, Greenwich, London at 8.00am.
  • Cyclists participating in this event are advised to arrive at least 30 minutes earlier for easy preparation and in time for a 15-minute safety talk for when cycling on the route.

Timetable:

  • Start Cycling (SC): 8.00am,
  • (2½ Hours)
  • Morning Break (MB): 10.30am (+ 15 Mins),
  • Continue Cycling: 10.45am,
  • (1¾ Hours)
  • Lunch Stop (LS): 12.30pm (+ 1 Hour),
  • Continue Cycling: 1.30pm,
  • (2½ Hours)
  • Afternoon Break (AB): 4.00pm (+ 15 Mins),
  • Continue Cycling: 4.15pm,
  • (2¾-4¾ Hours)
  • End Cycling (EC): 7.00pm-9.00pm.

What Is Required:

  • To be able to tackle this long-distance cycle route with ease, cyclists need:
  • To have the right sort of clothing for each types of weather
  • Bring their own safety equipment (crash helmets are essential)
  • Have a good level of fitness, high energy and skill of cycling;
  • The ability to cycle up and down steep hills;
  • To be prepared and have the right mind for each cycling day at all times;
  • Awareness of the dangers and precautions of some areas;
