Master Chest

Master Chests are above all three of the previous chests and contain the most valuable and rarest of the items and Pokemon along the whole route. These chests contain all the legendary Pokemon, the three best Poke Balls and 20 of the mega stones.

30 of these Master Chests are found in 30 towns along the route (1 in each town), which contain 90 items all together (3 in each chest).
