Poke Chests

At every milestone of reaching each city, town and village on the route, there's what is known as a Poke Chest to be found somewhere. These Poke Chests are just like the normal average treasure chest, only instead of a thousand gold dubloons there are three images on the inside of the lid when unlocked and opened and also a stack of money.

The three images show either one, two or three of 801 Pokemon types as well as 99 certain powerful items, such as poke balls and mega stones (making a total of 900 items - that's 300 chests for 300 places visited with 1 chest each). The images on the chest lid appear randomly in different ones, for example - one chest may contain 2 Pokemon and 1 item, another may contain 2 items and 1 Pokemon and another may just contain 3 Pokemon or 3 items.

The stack of money found in each of the chests is collected by the cylists in order to count it towards the three big charity events. Also in support of this event, other citizens who live in the 300 places donate money by putting into the chests for the cylists to collect.
