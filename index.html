Welcome to the official website for Cyclemon: the UK's first annual, long-distance cycle route in the theme of the beloved Japanese cartoon and video game series Pokemon!

This website gives clear information on where this poke-tastic challenge of a route takes you, what spectacular treasures need to be found, how this cycling event helps raise money for charity and just how much skill, determination and strength is needed to complete a daring trainer's journey around the length of Britian, to once again become the very best like no one ever was!
